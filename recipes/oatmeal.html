Oatmeal

Description

This is your basic oatmeal. It's pretty easy to cook.

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Cinnamon
  • Honey
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Combine all of the ingredients in a saucepan
  2. Bring the ingredients to a boil
  3. Take the heat down to a simmer and cook for 3-5 minutes. Allow to cool.
  4. Add desired toppings and serve.
