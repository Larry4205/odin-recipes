Pasta de sardine

Description

This is a pasta dish with sardines. Basically you take a can of sardines and mix it up with cooked pasta. It's pretty good.

Ingredients

  • Fettucinne
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Can of sardines (of course!)
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Saute the onion and garlie
  3. Add the sardines
  4. Mix the onions, garlic, and sardines with the pasta
  5. Add grated parmesan cheese.
