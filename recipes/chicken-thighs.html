Boneless skinless chicken thighs

Description

Basic baked chicken thighs with a spice rub. So easy I was able to teach my 72 year old dad to make it in a matter of minutes!

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs (right?)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic and onion powders
  • Paprika
  • Cumin

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a bowl
  3. Add olive oil and spices, and rub into chicken.
  4. Place chicken thighs in a single layer on a baking dish.
  5. Bake until cooked, about 30 minutes.
